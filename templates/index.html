How to Play

  • The goal of the game is to guess the secret word
  • The word is five letters long
  • You have six attempts to guess the word
  • If you guess the word, your score will be determined by how much time you took and the number of guesses

The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word

Examples

Green means that the letter is in the right position


Yellow means that the letter is in the word but not in the right position


Red means that the letter is not in the word at all
