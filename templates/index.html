How to Play

Guess the Word in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word

The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess to the word was

Examples

Green means that the letter is in the right position


Yellow means that the letter is in the word but not in the right position


Red means that the letter is not in the word at all
